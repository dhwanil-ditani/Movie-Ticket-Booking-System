Bala
Directed by Amar Kaushik. Starring Ayushmann Khurrana, Bhumi Pednekar & Yami Gautam. The story of a man who is balding prematurely and how he copes up with the situation.
Ujda Chaman
Directed by Abhishek Pathak. Starring Sunny Singh, Maanvi Gagroo & Saurabh Shukla. A balding 30-year-old bachelor in search of a beautiful wife is given a deadline to find one or remain celibate forever.
Housefull 4
Directed by Farhad Samji. Starring Akshay Kumar, Kriti Sanon & Kriti Kharbanda. Three pairs of reincarnated lovers reunite in the present day -- but the men wind up falling for the wrong women.
Terminator: Dark Fate
Directed by Tim Miller. Starring Mackenzie Davis, Arnold Schwarzenegger & Linda Hamilton. Sarah Connor and a hybrid cyborg human must protect a young girl from a newly modified liquid Terminator from the future.
Joker
Directed by Todd Phillips. Starring Joaquin Phoenix, Zazie Beetz & Robert de Niro. Failed comedian Arthur Fleck encounters violent thugs while wandering the streets of Gotham City dressed as a clown. Disregarded by society, Fleck begins a slow dissent into madness as he transforms into the criminal mastermind known as the Joker.
